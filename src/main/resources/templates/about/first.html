FIRST is a STEM education organization that is also an anagram for “For Inspiration and Recognition of Science and Technology”. We as FalconX participate in one of their four programs called FRC which is a competition that is the combination of excitement from sports with the rigorous challenges of science and technology. It consists of high-school student participants and under strict rules, limited resources, and an intense six-week time limit, teams of students are challenged to raise funds, design a team “brand,” build/work on teamwork skills, and build and program and industrial-size robot to play a difficult field game against like-minded competitors.
